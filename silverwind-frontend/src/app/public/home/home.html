What is PEGA?

Pega is a no-code/low-code platform that allows non-technical people to build complex apps. It empowers business process owners to work directly with developers to automate processes and improve customer experience.

Everything about Pega starts with the business need and evolves to continually improve. By using "Cases" to mirror manual processes, Pega allows teams to focus on solving customer issues rather than data entry.
